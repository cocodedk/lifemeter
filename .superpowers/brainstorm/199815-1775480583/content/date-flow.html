Date Entry Flow

How should picking a birthdate work? This affects the whole first-screen experience.

Option A — Two steps (keep current)
Choose your birth date
The dashboard updates live as soon as you pick a date.
Selected birthday: 15/3/1990
Change birth date

Continue →
User must tap Change birth date → pick date in dialog → then tap Continue to see results. Two taps minimum.

Pros

  • Explicit confirmation step
  • User controls when to apply

Cons

  • Extra tap every time
  • "Continue" is redundant once you understand the app
Option B — One tap (recommended)
Choose your birth date
Tap the button below to set your date. Results appear instantly.
Your birthday: 15/3/1990
Set birth date →
User taps Set birth date → picks date → dashboard appears immediately. One tap. On re-launch, saved date auto-loads results.

Pros

  • One tap to results
  • Pairs perfectly with "remember last date"
  • On re-launch: results are instant

Cons

  • Less explicit — first-time users might not see the change

I recommend Option B. With the saved birthdate feature, the app should open directly into the dashboard on subsequent launches — zero friction. Which do you prefer?
